Prologue

📖

Once Upon a Time…

✦ ✦ ✦

In a land not so far away, there lived a stubborn hero who made a few mistakes…

He was clumsy. He was awkward. He once lost an argument with a horse for twenty minutes — and the horse didn't even speak his language.

But there was one thing he was absolutely certain about —

"a girl named Mashka… who might decide his fate."
✦

This is that story. The horse has strong opinions. The frog is irrelevant.


Chapter I

🌙

The Enchanted Predicament

✦ ✦ ✦

Our heroine Mashka was wandering through an enchanted forest when she found herself completely, hopelessly, magically stuck.

A mischievous spell froze her path. The trees hummed. A frog offered advice. It was ignored (as frogs often are).

Only one person in all the realms could save her now.

✦

She waited. The forest held its breath…


Chapter II

🦸

The Rescue

And then — he appeared.
Through the enchanted fog, through the cursed brambles, through whatever that frog was doing — he came.
He broke the spell. He freed Mashka. She was safe.
But the magic had a price. The hero collapsed, silent, still… having given everything to save her. 💫

Chapter III

☕

The Revival

✦ ✦ ✦

A wise old wizard appeared from behind a bush and cleared his throat dramatically.

"He can be revived… with a coffee."

The wizard vanished. The frog nodded knowingly.

✦

Would you buy him a coffee?

Chapter IV

⏳

The Second Chance

✦ ✦ ✦

The hero stirred. He opened one eye. Then the other. He immediately tripped over the frog. Some things never change.

"What about… a second chance?"
✦

He waited. Three whole websites were built in silence.

The birds stopped singing. The frog got a part-time job. The horse wrote a strongly worded letter.

"After three websites… a follow back would be nice. I'll be less annoying haha"
"Which princess has made you 3 websites till now? 👀 Gimme another shot."
